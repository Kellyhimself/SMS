# Marketing Strategy for School Management System

## One-Line Description
"Streamline your school operations with our all-in-one School Management System—seamlessly manage students, fees, exams, and parent communications, both offline and online, tailored for Kenyan private schools."

## Key Selling Points

- **Comprehensive Management:** Handle student registration, fee payments, exam results, and report cards in one place.
- **Offline & Online Flexibility:** Choose between a one-time purchase (offline-capable) or a subscription-based cloud solution.
- **M-Pesa Integration:** Accept fee payments directly via M-Pesa, making transactions quick and secure.
- **Automated Notifications:** Keep parents informed with SMS and email alerts about fees, exam results, and school updates.
- **Scalable & User-Friendly:** Built with Next.js and Supabase, ensuring a modern, responsive, and easy-to-use interface.
- **Tailored for Kenyan Schools:** Designed specifically for the needs of Kenyan private schools, from low-fee to high-end institutions.

## Target Audience
- School owners, directors, and administrators of private schools in Kenya.
- Teachers and administrative staff seeking efficient school management tools.

## Call to Action
"Transform your school management today—try our system and see the difference!"
